# Welcome to Data360 Voice 🌍

Ask me about World Bank climate and development data. I can help you:

- **Search indicators** — find relevant climate, economic, or social metrics
- **Query data** — get values for specific countries, time periods, and indicators
- **Explore metadata** — understand what datasets and disaggregations are available

All responses include verified citations from the World Bank Data360 API.
